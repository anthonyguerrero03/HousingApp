About NinerHousingHub

Welcome to NinerHousingHub, your go-to spot for finding housing near UNC Charlotte. We're here to make your search for off-campus accommodations easier and more convenient. Whether you're a new student or looking for a change, we've got you covered! Our platform helps you browse through a variety of housing options, including apartments, sublets, and roommate listings, all close to campus. You can search based on your preferences, like location and budget, making it simple to find the right place for you. Need a roommate? No problem! We'll help you connect with other students who are looking for roommates too, making it easier to find someone you'll get along with. Plus, we've got plenty of resources and tips to help you out along the way. From FAQs to expert advice, we're here to support you through your housing journey. Join the Niner Housing Hub community today and find your perfect home away from home!
