The Edge Listing Description: The Edge Apartments, located at 1300 Varsity Lane, Charlotte, NC 28262, offers shared housing options for students. With an affordable rate of $724 per bedroom, this community provides convenient access to the University of North Carolina Charlotte, just steps away from campus. Amenities include a clubhouse, computer area, gym, pool, and volleyball court. The fully furnished apartments feature private bedroom and bathroom floor plans, with lease terms accommodating undergraduates and graduate students. Utilities such as trash removal, water, sewer, electricity, gas, and heat are included. Residents enjoy a vibrant community with various events and academic support services. Office hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 am to 6:00 pm, and Saturday, 10:00 am to 4:00 pm. Explore the lifestyle at The Edge Apartments today!

University Walk Listing Description: University Walk Apartments, situated at 1205 University Walk Circle, Charlotte, NC 28213, offers shared housing options for students seeking proximity to the University of North Carolina Charlotte. Priced at $919 per bedroom, these fully renovated and furnished apartments boast private bedrooms and bathrooms, providing convenience and comfort. Residents enjoy access to amenities such as a fitness center and basketball court, along with professional on-site management and maintenance services. Lease terms cater to both undergraduates and graduate students, with included utilities covering cable, trash removal, water, sewer, electricity, and heat. Office hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 am to 6:00 pm. Enjoy the UNCC lifestyle at University Walk Apartments!

Rush Apartments Listing Description: Rush Apartments, located at 9200 University City Blvd, Charlotte, NC 28213, offers luxurious shared housing options tailored for students at the University of North Carolina Charlotte. With rents ranging from $940 to $1,700, Rush provides a variety of floor plans including 1, 2, 4, and 5 bedrooms. Residents enjoy all-inclusive rental packages covering utilities, cable, and internet, along with access to amenities such as a fitness center, pool, and clubhouse. The community is designed to celebrate the spirit of Niner Nation, offering ample gathering spaces and private bedrooms with pillow-top mattresses. Office hours are Monday to Friday from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm, and Saturday and Sunday from 10:00 am to 5:00 pm. Experience the off-campus student living at Rush Apartments!
